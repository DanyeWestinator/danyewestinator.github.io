The Legend of Twitch Chat

48-hour game jam submission for VGDC's spring 2024 game jam! Theme was "Chat, is this real?"

A four-player game all on one keyboard! What could go wrong. In this retro inspired game, you play as a twitch chat, controlling the hero as you guide them to defeat an evil dragon who has been terrorizing the lands. Will you and your team be able to save the world from this mighty foe? I don’t know, just go for it I guess. You do you, I’m just a humble innkeeper.


Controls:

  • Player 1: Q / W
  • Player 2: C / V
  • Player 3: J / K
  • Player 4: - / =

Single Player Controls:

  • Move: WASD
  • Sword: H
  • Bow: J
  • Bomb: K
  • Fireball: L
