Zombieland

Slay the undead, while avoiding their cold & fatal embrace

Where it all began! My first playable .exe in unity, Winter Quarter 2019.
It was my second quarter with VGDC. I built systems like shooting and I think movement? It's adorable and earnest in the way only "MyFirstGame.exe" can be, so it naturally needed a WebGL build too.


Controls:

  • Move: WASD
  • Aim: Character aims where mouse is pointing
  • Shoot: LMB
  • Switch weapons: Scroll wheel
