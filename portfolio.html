Common'Hood

Steam - Fall 2022 / Xbox (Gamepass)- Spring 2023

Online multiplayer sandbox mode

  • From initial prototyping to final feature release in spring 2023
  • Worked with systems like custom skin selection, finding / joining games, porting all single player story environments to multiplayer sandbox maps, etc

We had to port over 3 years of legacy single-player codebase to a multiplayer paradigm, and do it in a way that would not break the existing story mode systems.
The senior developer and I created a clean unity project to prototype our implementation, then successfully transferred those ideas to the production codebase

Other features:

  • Built a custom localization importer / exporter from scratch
  • Built user requested quality-of-life tools, ensuring seamless gameplay
  • Handled bug fixes, controller support, patches, and other small but critical tasks, to maximize a development team of 2
