UC Irvine Class of 2023 - Zot Zot Zot!

I've wanted to make video games since I was around 7, so I went to UCI to study Computer Game Science. My first quarter, I joined the Video Game Development Club (VGDC), and immediately started working on real games. In my time there, I worked on tons of cool games with lots of cool people, worked on countless game jams and quarter-length projects, and fell in love with making games as an art form.

Transit and Bike Safety Champion

I wanted to make a real difference in my adopted city of Irvine, so two years ago, I was part of the group that co-founded Remake Irvine Streets for Everyone. RISE has had some recent wins, including increasing the frequency of the new Irvine Connect bus from 60 to 20 minutes between busses, as well as helping expand the city's Class IV protected bike lane network
