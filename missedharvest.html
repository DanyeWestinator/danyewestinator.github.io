Missed Harvest

Grab a friend, fly free, and escape the farm!

If you're too slow, you'll end up as the farmer's dinner!


KB/M Controls:

  • Move: WASD
  • BAWK: E
  • Sprint: Shift
  • Shoot: LMB
  • Jump: Space




Controller:

  • Move: Left stick
  • BAWK: X
  • Sprint: Left trigger
  • Shoot: Right trigger
  • Jump: A

(blue fullscreen button)
